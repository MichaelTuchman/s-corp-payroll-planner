# Solo S-Corp Payroll & Cash Planner

The Solo S-Corp Payroll & Cash Planner is an interactive tool for single-owner S corporations to plan one payroll scenario end to end — hours, wages, withholding, retirement contributions, and the cash left over afterward. It's built for solo contractors who'd rather use a tool that already handles SEP-IRA, Solo 401(k), and the tax nuances correctly than spend their own time getting them wrong first.

1. Full cash waterfall + Cash Health Status — the actual "should I be worried" answer, not just a paycheck calculator
2. And you don't just get that answer — you feel it happen: live sliders let you drag wage or retirement contribution. Watch Cash Health Status shift in real time
3. Solo 401(k)'s correct dual-bucket mechanic (deferral hits income tax withholding, not FICA)
4. Both SEP-IRA *and* Solo 401(k) supported, as mutually exclusive plans, or choose no retirement plan.
5. No accounts, nothing stored — privacy by architecture
6. Age 50+ catch-up correctly raises both the deferral and combined limits
7. Visual polish and accessibility (colorblind-safe, legible contrast)
8. Wage-base caps and surtax thresholds (SS, FUTA, Additional Medicare) tracked against YTD wages
9. Multi-scenario capture, compare, and CSV export
10. Faithful provenance from a real working spreadsheet, not a green-field guess
11. In-app Glossary and inline explanations
