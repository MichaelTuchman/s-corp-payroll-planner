# Changelog

All notable changes to the Solo S-Corp Payroll & Cash Planner will be documented here.

## [Unreleased]

### Changed

- Reorganized model results so Net Employee Paycheck concludes the employee-payroll section.
- Moved employer obligations and cash planning into a separate section.
- Renamed the snapshot status field to Cash Health Status.
- Formatted the snapshot cash-margin field as a percentage.

## [0.1.0] — 2026-07-14

### Added

- Single-tab payroll and cash-planning model.
- User-entered payroll, tax, unemployment, disability, leave, FUTA, and SEP assumptions.
- Employee paycheck calculation.
- Employer payroll-cost and required-deposit calculations.
- Available-cash margin and configurable Cash Health Status.
- Horizontal copy-ready payroll snapshot.
