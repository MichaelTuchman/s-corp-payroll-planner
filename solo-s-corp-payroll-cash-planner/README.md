# Solo S-Corp Payroll & Cash Planner

**Run payroll with confidence. Know what cash is truly available afterward.**

The Solo S-Corp Payroll & Cash Planner is a Microsoft Excel financial model for a single-owner S corporation with one W-2 employee: the owner.

It helps an owner model one payroll scenario, reserve every payroll-related obligation, and determine how much business cash remains genuinely available.

## What the planner does

- Converts planned billable hours and wage rates into expected revenue and gross payroll.
- Estimates employee withholding and employer payroll costs using editable assumptions.
- Separates the net paycheck from federal, state, local, unemployment, retirement, and operating-cash obligations.
- Calculates available cash and a configurable Cash Health Status.
- Produces a horizontal, copy-ready payroll snapshot for a future value-only payroll register.

## Product boundary

This workbook is designed for:

- one S corporation;
- one W-2 employee, who is also the owner;
- one payroll scenario at a time; and
- payroll and cash planning, not payroll processing or tax-return preparation.

It does not replace an accountant, payroll provider, or tax professional. Actual payroll results should be reconciled to the accountant or payroll provider's output.

## Workbook structure

The workbook currently contains one worksheet, **Planner**, organized into:

1. **Inputs**
2. **Default Tax Rates and Limits**
3. **Employee Payroll Results**
4. **Employer Obligations and Cash Planning**
5. **Copy-Ready Payroll Snapshot**

Blue cells are user inputs. Formula cells are black. The visible lookup table controls the Cash Health Status thresholds.
